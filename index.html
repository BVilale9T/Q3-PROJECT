How to Load Beep Card

To load your Beep card, visit any authorized loading station, vending machine, or partner convenience store. Follow the on-screen instructions or ask for assistance.

Passenger Tips and Reminders

  • Always line up properly at the ticket counters and entry gates.
  • Give priority to senior citizens, PWDs, and pregnant women.
  • Keep your belongings secure and be aware of your surroundings.
  • Follow station rules and regulations for a smooth journey.
